# Cosine similarity

Cosine similiarity to kąt pomiędzy dwoma wektorami w przestrzeni.

$$ 
similarity = 
\cos(\theta) = 
\frac
{\textbf{A} \cdot \b{B}}
{}
=
\frac
	{\sum_{i=1}^n{A_{i}B_{i}}}
	{\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^nA_{i}^2}\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^nB_{i}^2}} 
$$
